About Us

Welcome to GRPGHT, where FPS (First-Person Shooter) gamers come together to experience the thrill of live, competitive gaming. Our mission is to create a dynamic and immersive platform that brings FPS enthusiasts of all skill levels to compete, collaborate, and celebrate their passion for gaming in a real-world setting.

Founded by a dedicated team of FPS gamers, GRPGHT is more than just an event—it's a movement. We understand the adrenaline rush of a high-stakes match, the camaraderie of a team victory, and the constant drive to improve. That's why we've designed an arena where you can push your limits, hone your skills, and earn recognition and rewards for your efforts.

At GRPGHT, we host a variety of live events, tournaments, and challenges featuring popular FPS games. Whether you're into tactical shooters, battle royales, or classic arena shooters, you'll find your niche here. Our state-of-the-art anti-cheat measures ensure a fair and competitive environment, so you can focus on what truly matters: dominating the battlefield.

We're proud to have built a community of over 30 million gamers worldwide who share our passion for FPS gaming. From casual players looking for fun to hardcore competitors aiming for the top, everyone is welcome at GRPGHT. Join us, and be part of a community that's dedicated to elevating the live gaming experience.
